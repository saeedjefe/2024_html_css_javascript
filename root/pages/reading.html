The importance of Phrase Chunking in IELTS Reading Are you able to identify phrases in an IELTS reading passage? Did you know that no matter how difficult a passage is, it is only made up of three types of phrases, period,? Did you know that only one part of these phrases is almost always the answer to all the "sentence completion" and "diagram labeling" questions.
