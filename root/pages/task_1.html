The maps depict an industrial district in Norbiton town, with the top diagram illustrating the current lauyout and the bottom showing the proposed development. The zone is set to undergo massive changes:the introduction of a medical center, shops, a playground, and a school to the area, the replacement of factories by housing, the building of new roads leading into the farmaland, and the installation of a new roundabout at the south end of the area. The area is heavily industrilized at the moment, with one roundabout in the center, numerous factories lining the east road and the roundabout itself, a high way in the south, and isolated farmland in the north with no means of transportation. However, the factories are proposed to be replaced with housing, with the incoporation of shops and a medical center to the south-west and south-east of the central roundabout, respectively. Additionally, the construction of a school at the end of the east road and a play ground to the south of the river are two other plans decided for the Norbiton industrial area, while the north of the city will also experience some significant modifications. Indeed, to the north of the roundabout will stand a dead-end road which will lead into farmland after having crossed the north river.
